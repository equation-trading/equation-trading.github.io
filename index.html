Copula and Kernel Smoothing

Kernel Smoothing is a nonparametric technique to expose structural trend in the sample data. Copula is a mathematical tool to describe dependence structure among multivariate random variables through their marginal distribution function. These tools are widely used in risk calculation.

Equity Derivative Analytics

Supported instruments are vanilla European option, Asian option and barrier option. Validated models are traditional Black-Scholes, Heston, local volatility model with constant elasticity and mixed stochastic volatility and local volatility model.
